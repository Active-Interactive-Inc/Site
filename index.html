    • watch wall paint dry

    the best game of 2018 by far!
    imagine a game where you can watch the most entertaing moment happen: watch the paint on a wall dry. you get several different walls to watch the paint on it dry, and you don't have to buy any DLC to get them!!11!

    the amount of levels are endless!
    if you connect your platform to the internet, you'll be able to download custom levels from our servers! by clicking on the button below (and signing into your microsoft account), you will be able to upload your own walls! please just make sure to be family friendly, kids will want to play this game too!


    intense multiplayer action!
    if you have friends who are willing to compete with you, you can play the amazing multiplayer mode. the goal is to last the longest watching the wall paint dry. whoever drops out or leaves the room gets the high score. if your console overheats or you die while playing this, it's your own fault.
